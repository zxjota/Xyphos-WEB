100% Local Processing & Zero Cloud Data Collection

1. Information Handling

1.1 What We Do NOT Collect

VCAPHUB is designed to protect your privacy by default:

  • No cloud storage: We do not store your learning data, tasks, or statistics on any external servers
  • No learning tracking: We do not track your study patterns, task progress, or usage history externally
  • No personal data transmission: We do not require or transmit personal identifiers to any server
  • No network transmission: All operations occur locally on your device

1.2 Local Device Data

The application stores the following data locally on your device for functionality:

  • Learning tasks: Your tasks, subjects, descriptions, durations, and deadlines
  • Study records: Your study sessions, timers, and progress tracking
  • Learning statistics: Your daily, weekly, and monthly study analytics
  • App preferences: Your selected settings for the application

2. How Information is Used

All data stored locally on your device is used exclusively for:

  • Managing and organizing your learning tasks and subjects
  • Tracking your study sessions and Pomodoro timer data
  • Generating learning statistics and visualization reports
  • Maintaining app functionality and user interface preferences
  • Providing a consistent learning experience across app sessions

2.1 Local Data Processing

VCAPHUB processes all data locally on your device:

  • Task filtering and organization based on your criteria
  • Statistics calculation and chart generation
  • Pomodoro timer functionality and progress tracking
  • All algorithms execute entirely on your device

3. Data Security & Storage

3.1 Local Storage Architecture

All app data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using platform-specific secure storage mechanisms. This data is:

  • Encrypted by your device's operating system (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore, etc.)
  • Accessible only to the VCAPHUB application
  • Automatically deleted if you uninstall the app (depending on OS behavior)
  • Never transmitted to external servers or cloud services

3.2 Data Backup and Export

VCAPHUB provides optional data management features:

  • Local Export: You can export your data to a local file for backup purposes
  • No Auto-Sync: There is no automatic cloud synchronization
  • Manual Control: You have full control over any data sharing or export

4. Third-Party Services

VCAPHUB uses minimal third-party services:

  • App Store Analytics: Basic download statistics (provided by Apple App Store / Google Play Store)
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash data to improve app stability (optional, can be disabled in device settings)

These services operate under their respective privacy policies and do not receive your learning data, tasks, or study statistics.

5. Your Control & Rights

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • Full Data Ownership: All your learning data belongs exclusively to you
  • Local Management: Create, edit, and delete tasks and records as needed
  • Complete Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • No Accounts Required: No registration or login needed
  • Export Capability: Export your data to local files for backup

6. Children's Privacy

VCAPHUB does not knowingly collect or process data from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification due to its non-collection of personal data.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of VCAPHUB after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Significant changes will be noted in app update descriptions.
